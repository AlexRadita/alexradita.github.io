About Me

I’m Alex Radita, a Romanian driver in the European Rental Karting Championship. My karting journey began in early 2024, where I hit the top 200 out of over 10,000 drivers in my first session. By May, I’d climbed to the top 50 in the Junior category, and in July, I got a week to train in a race kart—though funding for pro racing wasn’t there yet. That experience fueled my drive, and I soon earned a spot in adult karts. In December 2024, I signed up for the European Rental Karting Championship, and now I’m all in to find sponsors to fuel my dream of going pro.

Achievements

  • Climbed to Top 50 in the Junior category in just a few weeks
  • Trained in a race kart for a week in July 2024, sharpening my skills
  • Will race for the European Rental Karting Championship in Summer 2025
